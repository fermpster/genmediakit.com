GEN eBooks

The GEN eBook is a digital collection of GEN articles on a trending topic, tool, or technology. It’s an interactive publication, approximately 25 pages, viewable on desktop and mobile devices. To view the GEN eBook, users must fill out a registration form—making eBooks a strong lead-generation tool for you. Each GEN eBook is exclusively sponsored. The sponsor receives highly visible branding, content links, impactful advertising positions, and the list of registrants for marketing purposes.

The eBook will also prominently feature information about your product or service and educational resources. We highly encourage and will provide guidance on developing interactive, multimedia formatted content including videos, podcasts, slide shows, polls, quizzes and expert tips.
